# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

### Personal Information

- Name
- Email address
- Account credentials
- Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers)
- Country
- Subscription information

### Technical Information

- IP address
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Device identifiers
- Login timestamps
- Usage analytics
- Session information
- Cookies

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Create user accounts
- Provide educational services
- Process payments
- Improve Platform performance
- Prevent fraud
- Protect copyrighted materials
- Detect suspicious activity
- Provide customer support
- Send important account notifications

---

## 4. Cookies

We use cookies to:

- Keep users logged in
- Remember user preferences
- Improve Platform performance
- Analyze Platform usage
- Enhance security

Users may disable cookies through their browser settings; however, some features of the Platform may not function properly.

---

## 5. Payment Security

Payment information is processed through secure third-party payment providers. We do **not** store complete payment card details on our servers.

---

## 6. Sharing Information

We do **not** sell personal information.

Information may be shared only with:

- Payment processors
- Cloud hosting providers
- Analytics providers
- Security providers
- Legal authorities where required by law

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for legitimate business purposes, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of our agreements.

---

## 9. User Rights

Where applicable under law, users may request to:

- Access their personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete personal information
- Restrict processing
- Object to processing
- Receive a copy of their personal data

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for children under **13 years of age** (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes become effective upon publication on the Platform. Continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

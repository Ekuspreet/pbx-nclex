# Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing **PBX Nursing**. Our platform provides immediate access to digital educational content, including NCLEX practice questions, study materials, and related learning resources.

## 1. Nature of Digital Products

All subscriptions, memberships, and question bank purchases are digital products delivered electronically. As no physical products are shipped, returns are generally not applicable once access has been granted.

## 2. Refund Eligibility

A refund may be requested within **5 calendar days** from the date of purchase only if:

- The subscription has not been substantially used.
- Fewer than **250 questions** have been attempted.
- No certificates or premium downloadable resources have been accessed.
- The request complies with this policy.

Refund requests made after **5 calendar days** from the purchase date will not be eligible for a refund.

## 3. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be provided for:

- Change of mind after purchase.
- Failure to pass the NCLEX examination.
- Lack of time or personal scheduling issues.
- Failure to use the purchased subscription.
- Purchases made using promotional offers, discount codes, or special campaigns.
- Violations of the Terms of Service, including account sharing or unauthorized use.

## 4. Technical Issues

If you experience technical difficulties preventing access to your account, our support team will make reasonable efforts to resolve the issue. If the issue cannot be resolved within a reasonable period and is determined to be caused solely by our platform, we may offer an appropriate refund or account credit at our discretion.

## 5. Refund Method

Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method used for the purchase.

Where the original payment method is unavailable or cannot accept refunds, an alternative refund method may be arranged at our discretion and in accordance with applicable laws.

## 6. Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are generally processed within **7–10 business days**. The time required for the amount to appear in your account may vary depending on your bank or payment service provider.

## 7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team and include:

- Full Name
- Registered Email Address
- Order ID or Transaction ID
- Reason for the refund request

Each request will be reviewed in accordance with this policy.

## 8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Return & Refund Policy at any time. Any updates will become effective upon publication on our website or application.

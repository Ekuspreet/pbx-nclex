# Cancellation Policy

## 1. Subscription Cancellation

Users may cancel their subscription at any time through their account settings or by contacting customer support.

## 2. Effective Date of Cancellation

Cancellation will become effective at the end of the current billing period. Users will continue to have access to all subscribed features until the subscription expires.

## 3. No Partial Refunds

Cancellation of a subscription does not entitle the user to a full or partial refund for the remaining portion of the active subscription period, except where required by applicable law or as provided in our Return & Refund Policy.

## 4. Auto-Renewal

If your subscription is set to renew automatically, you should cancel it at least **24 hours** before the next scheduled billing date to avoid being charged for the next billing cycle.

## 5. Re-Subscription

Users may subscribe again at any time after cancellation. Previous pricing, discounts, or promotional offers may not be available for future purchases.

## 6. Exceptional Circumstances

In exceptional situations, including duplicate payments, billing errors, or verified technical issues caused by our platform, cancellations and refunds will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

## 7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding cancellations, please contact our customer support team using the contact details provided on our website or mobile application.
